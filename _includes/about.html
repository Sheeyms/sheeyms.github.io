Our Program

Samarpan aims to achieve the following goals.

  • Rural Upliftment.
  • Skill Developement for the School / College Dropouts.
  • Skill Developement for Rural Women.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and skill developement to our community and inspire life long learning.
